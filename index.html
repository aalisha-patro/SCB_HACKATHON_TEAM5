COCA WOMEN WELLNESS

Our Socials

As we are based in the UK, we hold a mixture of online and ‘in-person’ meetups. As of June 2021, we hold regular fortnightly meetings, most of our ‘in-person’ meetups are held in the city of Manchester. However, we do host an annual group get-away to a destination of choice.

Our Resources

Coca Women Wellness hopes to provide useful digital resources that can help develop ‘mindful’ techniques and evoke inspiring conversation around several wellbeing topics. Further, as one of our core initiatives are to focus on the wellbeing of communities from the African & Caribbean diaspora – much of the resources showcased on this site has been curated by African & Caribbean influencers.

Our Community

Through our social media channels and forums (check out our discord), we aspire to foster an online community for women, particularly of the African & Caribbean diaspora. Our community strives to facilitate positive, thought-provoking discussion around topics and common socio-cultural factors that often impacts wellbeing.

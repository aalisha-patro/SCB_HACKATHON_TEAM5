Our mission

As an online platform, our mission is to host online resources designed to develop mindful practices which can help to boost overall wellbeing.

For greater ease and accessibility, we use digital mediums (e.g. videos, playlists, images) to showcase primary-sourced (e.g. recipes) and secondary-sourced content. Some of these include but are not limited to videos on physical exercise, mindful practices, recipes, music, social discussions and even meditations techniques.

We hope you find Coca Women Wellness a useful tool in your day-to-day life and hope to connect with you somewhere across our social media channels!

About the founder

Between 2015 and 2019 Sam attend the University of Greenwich, graduating with a 1st Class degree in BA Hons Public Relations & Communications. Once moving out and to another city in 2019, the pandemic hit, the world was in lockdown, and social interactions were almost completely limited to the screen.

Such developments encouraged Sam to create Coca Women Wellness, an online platform designed to provide online communities with resources focused on promoting mindfulness practices and wellbeing.

In line with her passion for African & Caribbean culture(s), health, and wellbeing, she decided to create the online platform - Coca Women Wellness. Her key aspiration and hopes for this site is to increase awareness around a variety of content that positively influences African & Caribbean culture, and the wellbeing of those within it.

Sam is currently working on several projects shedding more light, and developing the presence of, African Caribbean culture in media, health and other social spheres.
